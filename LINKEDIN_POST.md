## LinkedIn Post: AI-Powered GTM Signal Intelligence Platform

--- Start of Post ---

🚀 Excited to share my latest portfolio project: an **AI-Powered GTM Signal Intelligence Platform**! This tool is designed for early-stage B2B SaaS startups to cut through market noise and get actionable insights on competitors and market trends.

**What it does:**
- Monitors 4 AI coding tool competitors (Cursor, Windsurf, Codeium, GitHub Copilot) across changelogs, blogs, Reddit, and Hacker News.
- Uses Google Gemini 2.0 Flash to extract structured insights like feature launches, pricing changes, and community sentiment.
- Scores signals by impact (High, Medium, Low) using rule-based logic.
- Delivers daily digests via Telegram and a public web dashboard.

**Why I built it:**
As a Product/AI/Technical Business Analyst, I saw a clear need for founders and GTM teams to automate competitive intelligence without breaking the bank. This project demonstrates how to build a genuinely useful, deployable product on a lean, free-tier stack.

**Tech Stack Highlights:**
- **Backend:** FastAPI on Render
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **AI:** Google Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Frontend:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS on Vercel
- **Automation:** GitHub Actions

This project showcases a blend of product thinking, technical execution, and AI automation skills – exactly what I bring to the table for roles like Product Analyst, AI/ML Business Analyst, or GTM Engineer.
